📲 Install as App

    
To enable daily reminders, install this app on your home screen


    

      🔔 Notifications only work when Radha Jap is installed as a PWA (home screen app) — not from a browser tab.
    


    

      
1

      

        
Open in Chrome

        
Make sure you're using Google Chrome on Android. Other browsers may not support installation.

      

    


    

      
2

      

        
Tap the Menu (⋮)

        
Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) at the top-right corner of Chrome.

      

    


    

      
3

      

        
Tap "Add to Home screen"

        
Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" from the menu. Tap Add to confirm.

      

    


    

      
4

      

        
Open from Home Screen

        
From now on, always open Radha Naam Jap from your home screen icon — then reminders will work! 🙏
